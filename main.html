About Us

FarmFusion 2.0 is an AI-powered smart farming platform designed to help farmers make better decisions — from what to grow and when to water, to where to sell.

By combining AI, IoT, and real-time data, we provide intelligent crop and disease detection, smart weather alerts, live market prices, and multilingual voice support — all in one simple app.

Our mission is to make smart, sustainable farming accessible to every farmer — improving yield, income, and rural growth through data-driven insights.
